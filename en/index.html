    Prof. Arthur Araruna

    Professor and Researcher at UFC Quixadá

    Biography

    Arthur Araruna is a Professor and a Researcher at Campus de Quixadá of the Federal University of Ceará since 2013.

    His research focuses on Data Structures, but he also has an interest in Optimization and Programming Languages.

    He is currently enrolled in a PhD in Computer Science with the Masters and Doctorate in Computer Science Program from UFC, working on BST Computation Model and Dynamic Optimality.
